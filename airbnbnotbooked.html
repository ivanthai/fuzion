Description

The Space

The kitchen is fully equipped and furnished.

The living room has a large CozyHeat fireplace and state of the art 55' flat screen TV and sound bar.

Each bedroom has an ultra comfortable mattress, down comforter, and two terry bath robes for your comfort.

The backyard has a delightful al fresco dining patio, a sitting area with adirondack chairs around a large chimenea fire place, and a Weber grill.

The jetted hot tub is located in a very private and romantic nook surrounded by a wooden deck and mature willow trees.

A convenient laundry room with state of the art washer and dryer is connected to the main house through the car port.

Our wine country home is centrally located in the Sonoma Valley and only 20 min away from the Sonoma Town Square, less than 40 min from Napa valley. You can walk or bike to local wineries and restaurants or take a few minute drive to Glen Ellen and Jack London park.

We welcome children.
